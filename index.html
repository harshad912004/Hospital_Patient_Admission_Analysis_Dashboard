üìà Key Insights

  • High admission rates for older patients (age 60+), especially in cardiology and pulmonology.
  • Emergency admissions dominate in heart failure and chest infection cases.
  • Male patients have slightly higher readmission rates across departments.
  • Discharge success rate highest among patients with shorter admission periods.
  • Seasonal peaks observed in respiratory cases during winter months.
